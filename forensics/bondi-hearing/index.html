14. THE RESISTANCE RATCHET IN REAL TIME

EEDTM Application: The Resistance Ratchet predicts that when Mechanism 1 is blocked, elites shift to Mechanism 2 while preserving the extraction rate (Theta). Applied to congressional oversight:
M1: Direct Evasion (blocked by format)

The hearing format theoretically prevents simple refusal... members have 5 minutes, the witness is sworn in, cameras are rolling. But the 5-minute limit means M1 (silence) isn't necessary.

M2: Active Counter-Measures

When direct evasion might look bad, Bondi shifted to filibuster, personal attack, Garland deflection, district crime attack, etc. Each consumes the questioner's time while providing the appearance of engagement.

The Theta of Oversight: If we define "extracted accountability" as the ratio of binding commitments to questions asked, the Theta of this hearing is 0/89 = 0.00. If we use a looser definition (responsive answers / questions asked), Theta = 56/89 = 0.63. But 44 of those 56 responsive answers went to Republicans asking friendly questions. The accountability Theta for the minority party is 12/41 = 0.29... meaning the minority party "captured" only 29% of the answers it sought. The majority party captured 92%.
The Gamma of Oversight: Differential targeting ratio (Gamma) = GOP response rate / Dem response rate = 92/29 = 3.17x. For comparison, the subprime lending Gamma was 3.2x. Congressional oversight produces the same differential targeting ratio as predatory lending. IMPLICATIONS FOR THE PAPER

What the Grading System Proves:
  • Both parties average a C (52%/54%). The dysfunction is bilateral, not one-sided.
  • Democrats fail on Economy and Technique. They speechify instead of interrogating. The speech habit is their single biggest structural weakness (-1.7 vs GOP).
  • Republicans fail on Illocutionary Fidelity and Information Yield. They ask fake questions to a cooperative witness. Perfect technique applied to pseudo-directives.
  • Only 2 of 30+ members scored B+ or above. Both brought documents. Both used Younger-compliant technique. Both were attacked for it.
  • The Combined Effectiveness (QER × WAR) reveals the structural trap: the best Democratic questioners produce lower CE scores than mediocre Republican ones because witness cooperation is a function of political alignment, not question quality.
  • The format rewards the wrong behaviors: speechmaking (Dem), confirmation (GOP), evasion (witness). It punishes the only right behavior: evidence-based adversarial interrogation.
  • Gamma on the WAR scale = 3.9x... even larger than the raw responsive rate gap (3.17x), because the WAR includes good faith and commitments.
Recommended Structural Reforms (from the data):
  • Staff-led questioning: 30-minute blocks led by trained attorneys using Pozner-Dodd methodology. The Jan 6 Committee did this. It worked.
  • Enforcement mechanism: Real-time contempt referrals for non-responsive answers, like a court's power to instruct "answer the question."
  • Sequential examination: One examiner builds a complete Toulmin chain instead of 30 members each starting from zero.
  • Document-first protocol: All documentary evidence entered before oral questioning begins, preventing the witness from claiming unfamiliarity.
  • No yielded time: Eliminate the practice of yielding time to the witness for counter-attacks on prior questioners.
