In 1825, France deployed 14 warships to Haiti and demanded payment of 150 million gold francs (later reduced to 90 million) as the "price of freedom." Haiti paid for 122 years, ending in 1947. The mechanism: a percentage of government revenue extracted by a foreign power under military duress.

In 2025, Vectus Global deployed 200+ personnel and drones to Haiti under a contract granting it a percentage of government customs revenue for 10 years. The mechanism is structurally identical:
